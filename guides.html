Guides & Frequently Asked Questions

How long does recovery take?

Every incident is unique. Once we analyze your samples, most recoveries are completed within 24–72 hours.

Should we pay the ransom?

We advise against paying when possible. SafeDecrypt focuses on data restoration and legal compliance to minimize risk.

How can we prevent future attacks?

Implement regular backups, multi-factor authentication, and security awareness training. Our consultants can provide a tailored prevention plan.
